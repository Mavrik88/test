Who is QA and what does it do?

The job of a Quality Assurance engineer is more likely not to check quality (although this word is present in the name of the profession), but to control the correctness of all stages of development and the correct operation of the final product. Sounds a bit like a tester's task. But he is only engaged in checking the operation of the application and, according to the results (the presence of bugs and errors), accepts it or does not accept it. A QA engineer also monitors compliance with standards in software development, interacts with developers, designers, customers, preventing the very appearance of bugs and errors in software. True, our professions of a tester and QA are most often perceived as a single whole.
